Hi, you've logged onto Rotherham's longest established hair salon website. Our salon is located in the heart of Rotherham town centre. We are located on Corporation Street near the entrance to Tesco. There is good parking near the salon.

You're probably on this site, looking for a new hairdresser, so let's not beat about the bush, you are “Gold dust” to any business, and I would love you to try our salon.

So what can we offer to tempt you? Mmmmmm……
